Smashing Pumpkins Fansite!

The Smashing Pumpkins are considered "Grunge" or American Alternative Rock music from the 90's. They originated in the windy city of Chicago Illinois, They have been active since 1988 with a 6 year gap from 2000 to 2006.

Reasons I like The Smashing Pumpkins

  • They have different sounds for how you're feeling that day
  • Their music was monumental for the time and inspired many other great bands that I personally also like
  • There isn't a time a song comes on my playlist and I skip it, also I cannot think of a song I dislike
